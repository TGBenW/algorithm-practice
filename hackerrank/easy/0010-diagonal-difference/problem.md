**Summary**  
Given a square matrix, compute the absolute difference between the sums of its two diagonals.  

**Example**  
Matrix:
11 2 4
4 5 6
10 8 -12

Primary diagonal = 11 + 5 + (-12) = 4  
Secondary diagonal = 4 + 5 + 10 = 19  
Absolute difference = |4 - 19| = 15
